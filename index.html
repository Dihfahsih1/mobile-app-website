About Router App

Router app is an on demand service app that includes all services that are needed in homes, offices, schools, institutions and individuals.

Easy to Use

The app has been designed with a lot of simplicity in fuctionality which makes it easy for all clients to use it.

Best User Experience

It is vital to the long-term success of on-demand services because drivers, hosts, and other providers earn more money and stay more engaged when they do well on the platform.
The App supports users in getting more assignments and earning more. The company benefits from less turnover, greater economies of scale and more revenue.

Router client is an on demand service application that handles Taxi, Boda Boda, delivery,car rental, doctor, salon, cleaning, gas refill, fumigation, electrician, plumber, mechanic and massage services.
A client will have to request for the nearest service provider and they are served at their convenience hence making a payment for the service.
The Client can always contact the Support team of the app for any clarification
